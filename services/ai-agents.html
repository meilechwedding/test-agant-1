A recent build

A quoting agent that gave a fabrication shop its evenings back.

A 30-person fabricator was spending 10+ hours a week turning RFQs into quotes — copy-pasting from PDFs, looking up material costs, eyeballing margins. We built an agent that drafts the quote in 90 seconds, flags anything unusual, and routes it to the owner for a 30-second review.

“
I used to quote on Sunday nights. Now I review on Friday afternoons. The quote that took me an hour takes me a minute.
— Owner · custom fabrication shop

The problem

Quoting was a senior task that only the owner could really do — but it was eating ten hours a week of his time, and bottlenecking every deal.

  • RFQs sat in the inbox for 2–3 days
  • Inconsistent margins across quotes
  • Owner working evenings to catch up
  • Lost deals when prospects got faster bids elsewhere

The MOSTAI solution

A custom quoting agent trained on his pricing logic. It drafts the quote, applies the margin rules, and flags only the cases that actually need his judgment.

  • Average quote turnaround: under 10 minutes
  • Owner time on quoting: down 85%
  • Margin variance cut in half
  • Runs inside his existing email — nothing new to learn
