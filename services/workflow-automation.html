A recent build

An onboarding flow that ran without anyone touching it.

A growing service business onboarded every new client with a 14-step checklist managed in a shared spreadsheet. Steps got missed. Folders got forgotten. We replaced the whole thing with one quiet automation — kicked off the moment the contract is signed.

“
I haven't thought about onboarding in three months. That's the highest compliment I can pay a system.
— Operations lead · service business

The problem

Onboarding was a 14-step shared spreadsheet that lived in three people's heads. Steps got skipped, folders got forgotten, and the client felt the gaps.

  • Average onboarding: 6 days
  • Missed steps on roughly 1 in 4 clients
  • Three team members spending 2 hours each per client
  • Owner tracking it personally to make sure nothing slipped

The MOSTAI solution

A single automation that runs the entire flow the moment a contract is signed. Tools provision, docs generate, calendars sync, Slack channels open. The team sees only the exceptions.

  • Average onboarding: under 5 minutes
  • Missed steps: zero in 90 days
  • Team time per client: ~10 minutes
  • Clients now get a single, confident welcome message
