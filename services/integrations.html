One source of truth for a multi-location operator.

A 4-location operator ran on Shopify, QuickBooks, a custom inventory tool, Google Calendar, and a CRM that didn't talk to any of them. Reporting meant Tuesday mornings in spreadsheets. We built a quiet integration layer underneath — and the spreadsheets disappeared.

The problem

Eight tools, none of them talking. The same customer existed three times under slightly different names. Tuesday-morning reports were a manual spreadsheet exercise that took three hours.

  • Customer records duplicated across systems
  • Inventory numbers disagreed by 8–12%
  • Three hours a week reconciling reports
  • No single answer to "how are we doing?"

The MOSTAI solution

A custom integration layer between every system, with one canonical record per customer, real-time sync, and a single dashboard the owner actually trusts.

  • One source of truth across all 8 tools
  • Inventory variance: under 0.5%
  • Tuesday reporting time: zero — it's already there
  • The team kept using the tools they already knew
